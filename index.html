About me

Hello! My name is Crispin: I am a data analayst with the mobile game studio MAG Interactive in Stockholm, and I completed my PhD on radio pulsar polarisation at the University of Manchester. I am passionate about understanding how the world works, and inherently curious about everything! I have particular interest in modelling both physical phenomena and recently, motivated by my work in gaming, human behaviour.
